# Avenger Guild

**Liked Guilds:** Barbarian, Chaos Association, Sorcerer

**Disliked Guilds:** Inquisitor, Law Association, Paladin

## Ranking

### Entry

You must go through ordeals of agony and anguish, and dwell amongst the chaos of the world. You must join political protests, attend violent music concerts, befriend vagabonds and artists, try out psychoactive drugs, get into street fights, and walk into forests that are on fire. The GM may also suggest alternative or additional ordeals. Finally, you will be admitted to the Avenger Guild.

### Increase

Frequently practice the skill of Avenging, frequently show it off to others, write or perform works that show great inner angst and conflict, break down the willpower of adherents of Law. Kill those who cause massive wide-scale deception.

### Decay

Fail to speak up when a member of the Law Association is around, fail to speak out in favor of Avenging, do not use the skill of Avenging, respect the beliefs of adherents of Law, allow anyone to believe something that you know is false.

### Rank 10 Responsibilities

Start up acts of Avenging in the streets, organize political protests, organize violent music concerts.

### Rank 20 Responsibilities

Evaluate new members, protect guild houses, start street fights, speak at political protests, perform at violent music concerts.
