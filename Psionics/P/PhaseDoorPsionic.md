[Phase Door, Psionic](/Psionics/P/PhaseDoorPsionic.md) - Circle 7, Nomad, Psychoportation

- **Circle:** 7
- **Category:** Nomad
- **Discipline:** Psychoportation
- **Display:** Visual
- **Map Range:** 0 feet
- **Map-less Range:** Melee
